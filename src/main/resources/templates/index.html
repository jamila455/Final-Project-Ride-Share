Share a Ride. Save Time. Stay Connected.

This Campus Ride Share app helps MCC students coordinate rides between campuses and around the Omaha metro area.

Offer a ride as a driver or join an existing ride as a passenger — safely and conveniently within the MCC community.

Browse Available Rides Offer a Ride

How it Works

  • • Register with your MCC email address.
  • • Create a ride to any MCC campus or metro location.
  • • Set the date, time, and number of seats.
  • • Other students can sign up as passengers.

Why Use Ride Share?

Save money, reduce stress, and build community:

  • • Save on gas and parking.
  • • Coordinate inter-campus travel (Fort, South, Elkhorn, etc.).
  • • Support classmates who don’t have reliable transportation.
